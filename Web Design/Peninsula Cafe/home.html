What is Peninsula?

With Venezuelan roots and traditions, Peninsula is the perfect place in Boston to taste the amazing tropical delicacies from the best places in Venezuela. The various dishes and desserts found in Venezuela's gastronomy are within your reach by visiting our restaurant in South Boston. We have desserts tha you'd surely enjoy!

About Us

Peninsula is a family owned restaurant and is operated by Roberto and Catherine Reyes, with the expert recipes created by Mamá Tarina. Together we have over 15+ years of experience in the restaurant business and we count with an excellent of group people who make will make your dining experience one to remember. Our mission is to become a place in which you can enjoy good food with good people, surrounded by the culture who created our wonderful dishes.
