Who Are We?

Peninsula is a family owned restaurant and was founded by Roberto and Catherine Reyes, with the expert recipes created by Mamá Tarina. Together we have over 15+ years of experience in the restaurant business and we count with an excellent of group people who make will make your dining experience one to remember. Our mission is to become a place in which you can enjoy good food with good people, surrounded by the culture who created our wonderful dishes.

Roberto & Jhonatan are the owners of Peninsul Café. Their goal is to alwaya make the best of traditional Venezualn dishes, and always improving dishes so that you can get all those tasty flavours. They're more recent new dish is the Oat-Fororo Arepa, a mix between dough based on Oats and Fororo, a famous cereal-like ingredient in Venezuela.
